    tslearn.utils.ts_zeros¶

    tslearn.utils.ts_zeros(sz, d=1)[source]¶

    Returns a time series made of zero values.

    Parameters
    szint

    Time series size.

    dint (optional, default: 1)

    Time series dimensionality.

    Returns
    numpy.ndarray

    A time series made of zeros.

    Examples

    >>> ts_zeros(3, 2)  
array([[0., 0.],
       [0., 0.],
       [0., 0.]])
>>> ts_zeros(5).shape
(5, 1)
